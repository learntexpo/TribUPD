The Tata Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate that was established in1868 by Jamsetji Tata.
Headquartered in Mumbai, it is India's largest conglomerate, with products and services in over 150 countries, and operations in 100 countries across six continents.
The group is involved in various businesses, including automotive, chemicals, defence, electronics, FMCG, jewellers, home appliances, salt, steel, cement, tea, airlines, aerospace, consultancy, electric utility, electric power, finance, hospitality, hotels, information technology, retail, e-commerce, real estate, and telecommunications.
As of FY 2023, the Tata Group had a revenue of ₹12 lakh crore (US$150 billion) and employed 1,028,000 people. The group has 29 publicly listed companies with a combined market capitalisation of ₹24 trillion (US$300 billion) as of July 2023.
