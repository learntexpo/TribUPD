Ratan Tata's Time Line

1937: Birth and Early Life

id="1937: Birth and Early Life"

  • Ratan Tata is born on December 28, 1937, in Bombay (now Mumbai), India.
  • Raised by his grandmother and later by his aunt after the loss of his parents at a young age.

1950s-1960s: Education and Early Career

id=""

  • Completes his early education in Mumbai.
  • Pursues higher education in the United States, earning degrees from Cornell University and Harvard Business School.
  • Begins his career by working on the shop floor of Tata Steel, gaining firsthand industrial experience.

1991: Chairman of Tata Sons

id=""

  • Assumes the role of Chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group, in 1991.

2000s: Expansion and Globalization

  • Leads the Tata Group through a period of expansion and globalization, diversifying into various industries, including automobiles, steel, information technology, and telecommunications.

2008: Acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover
